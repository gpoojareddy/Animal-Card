Octopus

Octopuses are sea animals famous for their rounded bodies, bulging eyes, and eight long arms. They live in all the world's oceans but are especially abundant in warm, tropical waters.

  • Scientific Name: Octopus Vulgaris
  • Size: 0.3-4m (1-13ft)
  • Weight: 5-75kg (11-165lbs)
  • Top Speed: 43km/h (27mph)
  • Life Span: 2-15 years
  • Favourite Food: Crabs

Generally, most species of octopus have no internal or external skeleton which means it is able to squeeze itself into tight places. The octopus is known to be one of the most intelligent of all the invertebrates and the octopus uses this to its advantage mainly using elaborate disguise.
